What Is AI for Social Good?

A high-energy weekend where Bay Area creatives, technologists, and changemakers come together to build AI-powered solutions for real-world challenges.

Whether you're new to AI or a seasoned pro, this is your chance to design for impact, collaborate with mentors, and bring your ideas to life in an inclusive, purpose-driven environment.

This isn't just a hackathon. It's your launchpad for ethical innovation, career growth, and meaningful change.

About BRIDGEGOOD

BRIDGEGOOD is an award-winning 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN 27-0720655) that empowers students and job seekers of color through tuition-free design education, mentorship from industry professionals, and career development rooted in empathy and equity.

Since 2009, BRIDGEGOOD has connected thousands of emerging creatives to paid opportunities, real-world experience, and the confidence to pursue meaningful careers in design and technology.

All proceeds from Amplify Together directly fund these programs—helping build creative futures, stronger communities, and lasting social impact.
